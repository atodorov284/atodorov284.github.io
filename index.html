About Me

Personal Statement

I am committed to combining rigorous academic study with practical, hands-on experience. As I am pursuing my academic journey within the field of AI, I also work as an AI Engineer and a Teaching Assistant. These roles are deeply interconnected; my engineering position allows me to test and apply theoretical frameworks in a professional context, while my teaching duties for several courses reinforce my own mastery of the subject matter by teaching it to others. Combining these academic, professional, and teaching commitments has sharpened my organizational abilities and has been fundamental to my growth.

Research Specialization

My research interests lie at the intersection of computational learning, mathematical optimization, and probability theory. I specialize in meta-learning and multi-task RL, with particular focus on continual learning and model overparameterization. My interests combine probability theory, optimization techniques, and applied Bayesian statistics to develop more efficient, robust, and adaptable learning systems.

Multi-Task Reinforcement Learning Smooth and Non-Smooth Convex Optimization Continual Learning Bayesian Statistics

Industry Experience

In the industry, I focus on developing practical AI solutions across various domains. My expertise includes implementing computer vision systems for production line validation, OCR and document understanding pipelines, and designing agentic workflows for business process automation through strong DevOps practices and cloud infrastructure.

OCR & Computer Vision in Manufacturing Document Understanding with NLP Agentic Systems for Business Process Automation
